Toggles

Default toggle can be adapted with the modifiers, it could end up looking like this...

toggle toggle--size toggle--colour toggle--label-style

Standard Toggle

With text inside

.toggle--text-inside

The logic may seem a bit weird but just remember, to turn the switch ON we have to click 'OFF'

As you can see, it requires short words, try YES NO, ON OFF, | O. For longer words try the .toggle--text-outside below

Positive and negative

.toggle--positive-negative

This has been stringed with the .toggle--text-inside class.

Positive and neutral

.toggle--positive-neutral

With text outside

.toggle--text-outside

Note how the label text have been swapped, we now want clicking 'OFF' to select OFF

Place a label on the left that toggles both checkboxes

.toggle--label-on-left

Large Toggle

.toggle--lrg

Small Toggle

.toggle--sml
